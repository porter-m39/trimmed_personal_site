Porter Morgan

I'm a fifth year Math PhD student at the University of Massachussets Amherst, advised by Inanc Baykur. I study low-dimensional topology, specifically 4-manifolds. I'm interested in Lefschetz fibrations, nonorientable 4-manifolds, and exotic smooth structures.

Contact: pamorgan AT umass DOT edu

  • Belted sum decompositions of fully augmented links. with Brian Random, Dean Spyropoulos, Rolland Trapp, and Cameron Ziegler. New York J. Math, 31 (2025):1-42, 2024. [PDF]
