Porter Morgan

I'm a sixth year Math PhD student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, advised by İnanç Baykur. I study low-dimensional topology, specifically 4-manifolds. I'm interested in Lefschetz fibrations, nonorientable 4-manifolds, and exotic smooth structures.

Contact: pamorgan AT umass DOT edu
